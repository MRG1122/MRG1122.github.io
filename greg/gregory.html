Check out my Job!

I currently work for the Pitt football team as a recruiting assistant. It has been an awesome experience and I have learned a lot about the business side of college football. In my role, I assist with scouting and evaluating high school athletes that Pitt is interested in. During the season, I get to stand on the sidelines for the games and entertain recruits who are invited to watch the games. I have also had the chance to meet some of the Steelers players, as they share a facility with the Pitt team. This job has been a really fun experience and one that I will always remember.
